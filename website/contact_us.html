Contributor Guidelines

Join our open-source community and help build the future of AI-powered news. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, there's a place for you here.

1

Getting Started

Fork our repository, read the README, and set up your development environment. Join our Discord for real-time support and discussions.

2

Choose Your Area

Pick from frontend (React Native), backend (Node.js), AI/ML (Python), design (Figma), documentation, or testing. All skills are valued!

3

Code Standards

Follow our ESLint config, write tests, use meaningful commit messages, and ensure accessibility. Quality over quantity always.

4

Submit & Review

Create detailed PRs, respond to feedback constructively, and celebrate when your contribution gets merged!
